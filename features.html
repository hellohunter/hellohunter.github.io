FEATURES

List of features offered by HelloHunter, Telemarketing Platform

HelloHunter Unique Dialing Platform

Learn how Hello Hunter's unique telephone technology brings to big corporation auto & predictive dialing systems to small and medium businesses.

Once the call has been answered, the autodialer either plays a recorded message or connects the call to a live person. HelloHunter has a "Live Person Confirmation" feature allowing the contact to indicate that they have heard and understood the message by pressing a button on their handset. All interaction are recorded in a comprehensive reporting system.

Voice broadcasting allows telemarketers, politicians, and non-profit organizations to reach thousands of phone numbers with an extremely low cost per impression. Your recorded message can be immediately delivered to businesses or households with a click of the mouse.

Features


IVR Flow

Complex IVR trees can be designed to collect information from the contact and includes recording, multi-choice, extension dialling, and conferencing nodes.

 

HIGH CAPACITY

Tests on a mid-range server resulted in 1 million calls in just under 5 hours. Multi-Server systems will result in much higher capacity.

 

VOICEMAIL DETECTION

Voicemail Detection enhances the HelloHunter system by detecting if the call has been answered by voicemail, and leave a different message, or simply hang-up.

 

REALTIME REPORTING

Capacity monitoring and other key performance indicators are reported in realtime, with downloadable reports and enabling drill-down to individual survey results.

 

SCALABLE / HIGH AVAILABILITY

Multiple servers can be clustered to create a highly fault tolerant and scalable system.

 

ADAPTIVE WEB PAGES

Web management interfaces adapt to the size of browser being used, making HelloHunter controllable by smart phone, tablet, laptop and desktop devices.

 

USER FRIENDLY

Intuitive web-based customer and management interfaces requiring minimal operator training.

 

API INTEGRATION

Numerous API's are available to allow straightforward integration with third party applications.

 

SMS BROADCASTING

SMS integration into HelloHunter allows mass broadcasting of text messages to cell-phones.

 

CONTACT ACQUISITION

A sign up page is provided to allow the community to register their phone details online with HelloHunter and select which type of alerts they want, for instance school closures through to disaster and evacuation alerts.
