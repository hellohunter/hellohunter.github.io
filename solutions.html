Solutions

HelloHunter is a versatile platform, and can be used not only for reverse dialer broadcasting, but also in a variety of other roles including telemarketing, live lead generation and conferencing. Some applications are listed below.

Schedule a demo today

Mass Emergency Broadcasting

Mass Emergency Broadcasting is the main role for HelloHunter, e.g. Where there is a necessity to warn large numbers of people in a short space of time, such as weather warnings to more mundane warnings such as school closures and general community information.

SMS Broadcasting

SMS Messaging to send text messages to mobile devices containing marketing, informational and warning messages.

Phone Polling, Surveys & Voting

Ring large numbers of people and present IVR options for either polling their opinions, interactive surveys, or taking their vote and record the results.

HelloHunter comes with a variety of IVR nodes to collect results which can then be exported to Excel or CSV file. HelloHunter can also record a message from the contact.

Outbound Call Conferencing

Conferencing using outbound calling to initiate a conference to participants at a pre-arranged time.

Payment Reminders & Debt Control

Customers can be automatically reminded at intervals that they owe money, and an IVR menu presented to talk to the finance department or passed to a credit card capture IVR to pay over the phone.

Payment reminders and debt hunting via voice broadcasting is a cost effective way of contacting debtors, and will often pay for itself in a short space of time.

Live Lead Generation & Telemarketing.

Telemarketing and “Press One” campaigns are viewed as effective sales and marketing tools. A contact list is uploaded, and a campaign configured to call the list with a short introductory message to the product or service in question, followed by “To talk to a live person, press one now” HelloHunter will then bridge or live-transfer the call to one of your sales agents via your IP-PBX.
